WEDWay Peoplemover

The WEDway PeopleMover is an urban mass transit PeopleMover system attraction. Designed as an urban mass-transit system of the future, vehicles take passengers on a grand circle tour of the realm of Tomorrowland that provides elevated views of several other attractions.
