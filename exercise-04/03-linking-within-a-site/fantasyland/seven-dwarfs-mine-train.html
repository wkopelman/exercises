Seven Dwarfs Mine Train

Seven Dwarfs Mine Train is a steel roller coaster. It version opened to the public on May 28, 2014, as part of a major park expansion called New Fantasyland.

The ride is themed to Walt Disney's 1937 film Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs, the first full-length, traditionally-animated feature film.
