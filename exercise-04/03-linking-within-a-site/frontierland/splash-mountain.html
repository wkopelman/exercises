Splash Mountain

Splash Mountain is a log flume with a theme based on the 2009 film, The Princess and the Frog.

Each installation begins with a peaceful outdoor float-through that leads to indoor dark ride segments, with a climactic steep drop into a "briar patch" followed by an indoor finale. The drop is 52.5 feet (16.0 m).
